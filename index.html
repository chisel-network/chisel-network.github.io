Chisel

An optical slice of the wide-area network

Chisel is a system that dynamically carves slices of the wide-area network (WAN), enabling an end-to-end network slicing abstraction. Chisel creates all optical slices between endpoints in the wide-area to avoid queueing and congestion delays inherent in packet switched paths in WANs. Chisel reduces the hardware costs of allocating slices by using all optical paths.
